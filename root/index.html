Murofast is synonymous with quality and trust

Our main commitment has always been to offer you the best service and quality in all our products, which is why for more than 20 years we have established ourselves as one of the main marketing companies of light construction materials in Tijuana and the Baja California region.

We have logistic capacity and qualified personnel to guarantee the ideal solutions for your light construction material needs, offering product lines for all types of requirements, such as drywall, insulation, ceilings, and light anti-seismic systems.

Our competitive advantage is having our own factory to produce galvanized metal profiles at any custom lengths to fit your construction needs, ensuring the best quality at fair prices.
